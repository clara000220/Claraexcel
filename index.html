About

A trainer who actually trains.

For over a decade, Clara has helped Malaysian professionals stop fighting with their spreadsheets and start using them as decision-making tools. Her courses are HRDC-accredited and built around the 60 / 25 / 15 principle — 60% hands-on practice, 25% live demo, 15% Q&A.

Every example is grounded in Malaysian business context: real RM, real KL invoices, real Selangor payroll. Participants leave with workbooks they can use on Monday morning, not generic textbook drills.

From finance teams at GLCs to operations managers at SMEs, Clara has trained engineers, accountants, HR coordinators, marketers and CEOs — anyone whose work runs on data.

  • HRDC-accredited (TTT-certified) corporate trainer
  • Microsoft Excel — formulas, PivotTables, Power Query, VBA
  • AI for business — ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini
  • Agentic AI — multi-step AI agents for office workflows
  • Bilingual delivery: English & Bahasa Malaysia
