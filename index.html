Courses

Excel · Beginner

Microsoft Excel — Beginner

2 days · New to Excel

For office staff who use Excel daily for data entry and want to gain real speed and confidence.

What you'll learn

  • Excel interface, ribbon and navigation
  • “Tekan-Tarik-Tarik” range-selection mnemonic
  • Core formulas: SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT, IF, ROUND
  • Cell formatting, borders, conditional formatting
  • Sorting, filtering, freeze panes, AutoFill
  • Charts: column, line, pie — when to use which
  • Printing layouts, headers/footers, page setup

You'll leave able to: confidently navigate Excel and build clean, formatted spreadsheets without asking for help.

Excel · Intermediate

Microsoft Excel — Intermediate

2 days · Comfortable with basics

For users ready to manage real datasets, build summary reports and stop doing repetitive work.

What you'll learn

  • Advanced logic: IF, AND, OR, IFS, nested formulas
  • SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS, MAXIFS
  • VLOOKUP, XLOOKUP and INDEX/MATCH cross-sheet lookups
  • Named ranges and structured references
  • PivotTables and PivotCharts from scratch
  • Data validation, dropdown lists, dependent lists
  • Conditional formatting with formulas, sparklines, data bars
  • Importing CSV/text and cleaning data

You'll leave able to: build reusable templates and analyse datasets like a power user.

Excel · Advanced

Microsoft Excel — Advanced

2 days · Power user level

For analysts and managers handling large datasets, automation and complex modelling.

What you'll learn

  • Power Query: get, transform, merge, refresh
  • Power Pivot and the Excel Data Model
  • DAX fundamentals for measures and KPIs
  • VBA macros: recording, modifying, buttons, triggers
  • What-If: Scenario Manager, Goal Seek, Data Tables
  • Dynamic arrays: FILTER, SORT, UNIQUE, SEQUENCE, LAMBDA
  • Financial functions: NPV, IRR, PMT, FV
  • Interactive dashboards with slicers and form controls

You'll leave able to: automate repetitive work and build executive-ready dashboards from raw exports.

Excel + AI

Excel with AI

1 day · Intermediate

Use AI assistants to cut Excel work in half — write formulas faster, analyse data smarter and build reports with less effort.

What you'll learn

  • Microsoft Copilot inside Excel — what it does and doesn't do
  • ChatGPT for formula generation, debugging and explanations
  • Claude for spreadsheet analysis and data summaries
  • AI-powered data cleaning and reformatting prompts
  • Generating charts and pivot summaries through AI
  • Privacy & data-handling best practice (what NOT to paste)

You'll leave able to: hand off the tedious Excel work to AI and focus on the analysis.

AI · All Levels

AI in Workplace Productivity

1 day · All levels

For anyone who wants AI to handle the routine office work — emails, minutes, research, dashboards, finance extraction, HR shortlisting, and even building small apps.

What you'll learn

  • ChatGPT for emails, replies and meeting minutes
  • Claude for long-document summaries and research
  • Google Gemini inside Docs, Sheets and Gmail
  • Google AI Studio for prototyping with Gemini
  • NotebookLM for deep research on your own documents
  • Perplexity for AI-powered web search & citations
  • Prompt design for office workflows (the “CRAFT” method)
  • Turning data into narrative reports
  • Image AI for slides, posters and social
  • AI-generated dashboards & visual summaries
  • Workflow automation across apps (Zapier / Make / n8n style)
  • Finance: receipt & invoice data extraction with AI
  • HR: resume extraction & candidate shortlisting
  • App generation with AI (no-code / low-code builders)
  • Picking the right AI for the right job

You'll leave able to: save 5–10 hours a week on routine office work and unlock real AI use-cases for finance, HR and operations.

Google Sheets

Google Sheets — Beginner to Advanced

2 days · All levels

For teams on Google Workspace and consultants who need cloud-based, collaborative spreadsheets.

What you'll learn

  • Google Sheets interface, shortcuts and tabs
  • Formulas familiar from Excel + unique ones (QUERY, IMPORTRANGE, ARRAYFORMULA, FILTER)
  • Real-time collaboration: share, comment, suggest, version history
  • Data validation, dropdown lists, protected ranges
  • Charts, dashboards and interactive filter views
  • Connecting Google Forms to Sheets
  • Importing from CSV, websites and other Sheets
  • Pivot tables and conditional formatting in Sheets

You'll leave able to: use Google Sheets from foundations to power-user techniques in a cloud-first team.

Apps Script

Google Apps Script Automation

2 days · Intermediate

For Google Sheets users who want to automate workflows with simple JavaScript — no full developer background required.

What you'll learn

  • What Apps Script is and how it powers Google Workspace
  • Recording macros in Sheets and inspecting the code
  • JavaScript basics for spreadsheets
  • Triggers: time-based, on-edit, on-form-submit
  • Sending emails from Sheets automatically
  • Connecting Sheets to Gmail, Drive, Calendar
  • Building custom menus, buttons and sidebars
  • Using AI to generate and explain Apps Script code

You'll leave able to: build automated Google Workspace workflows that run themselves on a schedule.
